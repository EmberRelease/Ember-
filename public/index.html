What Ember is

Not a feed, not a forum, not a therapist. Ember is a calm bridge between what you feel, what you can name, and what you may be ready to bring into your life.

Ease of use rubric

Clarity Users know what Ember is, how to begin, and how to reset.
Calmness The interface lowers pressure instead of feeling busy or transactional.
Distinctiveness It feels unlike a default chatbot shell or support widget.
Control Memory and thread boundaries are visible and understandable.
Warmth The design feels human-centered without becoming saccharine.
Flow Writing, reading, and reflecting all feel effortless.
Reflective space

Become more real, then more reachable.

Ember should feel like entering a room, not opening a chatbot. The interface slows the pace, softens the pressure, and leaves enough space for thought to become language before it becomes movement.
