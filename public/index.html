What Ember is

Not a feed, not a forum, not a therapist. Ember is a calm bridge between what you feel, what you can name, and what you may be ready to bring into your life.

Ease of use

To feel 5/5, Ember should feel calm, obvious, warm, and unlike a generic chat widget. The interface should invite honesty without demanding performance.

Reflective space

Become more real, then more reachable.

Ember should feel like entering a room, not opening a chatbot. The interface slows the pace, softens the pressure, and leaves enough space for thought to become language before it becomes movement.
